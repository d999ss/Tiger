BIOSTIMULATORY FILLER

The longest lasting FDA-approved dermal filler that provides both immediate correction and natural collagen stimulation.

The “Must-have” product for your aesthetic practice.

Bellafill is the longest-lasting, FDA-approved, dermal filler on the market. The collagen gel provides an immediate, natural correction while the microsphere technology stimulates your own collagen creation for an elegant, refreshed looking you! Patients are demanding a longer lasting alternative to their short-acting HA fillers and only Bellafill can best address this need.

  • Address the #1 Patient Need – 90% of patients are interested in a long-lasting dermal filler1
  • Create Patient Loyalty – 82% of patients would switch providers for a long-lasting dermal filler1
  • Grow your practice beyond dermal fillers – 88% of patients would spend more money on other aesthetic procedures with the practice that provided them with a long-lasting dermal filler1

Bellafill is the longest-lasting filler.

Bellafill is the most studied dermal filler on the market.2, 3 With over 1,542 patients across multiple clinical studies,2, 3 Bellafill has proven itself time and time again to be safe and effective. In fact, Bellafill's 12-year Postmarket Surveillance data shows a consistently low adverse event rate of 0.11% that is comparable to other HA fillers.4, 5

In a Clinical Study.

Bellafill was shown to stimulate both Type III and Type I – the ideal types of collagen for natural and long-lasting correction. The natural and long-lasting result is what gives Bellafill its unprecedented patient satisfaction.

  • 87% satisfaction rate in Bellafill’s 5-year clinical study.6

Bellafill is the only FDA-approved dermal filler to safely and effectively treat acne scars.

Bellafill fades acne scars by adding volume to them, under the skin. This brings them to the same level as the surrounding skin and keeps them there long-term. Unlike hyaluronic acid fillers, which often dissolve within a few months, Bellafill creates a lasting improvement in your skin. It restores rather than just fills for natural-looking, enduring results.

  • 83% of patients who used Bellafill to treat their acne scars were still satisfied with their results at the 1-year mark.5
