THE SCIENCE

How Avéli™ Works

Cellulite dimples are caused by fibrous bands (septa) that pull down on the skin. Avéli precisely targets and releases these bands for smoother-looking skin.

🎯

1. Identify

Your provider identifies the specific septa bands causing your cellulite dimples using advanced visualization.

✂️

2. Release

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Your Questions Answered

How is Avéli different from other cellulite treatments?

Avéli is the first and only FDA-cleared procedure that directly targets and releases the fibrous bands (septa) that cause cellulite dimples. Unlike creams, massages, or other treatments that work temporarily on the surface, Avéli addresses the root structural cause.

How long does the procedure take?

The Avéli procedure typically takes 30-60 minutes, depending on the treatment area and number of dimples being addressed. It's performed in-office under local anesthesia.

What is the recovery like?

Most patients experience minimal downtime and can return to normal activities shortly after the procedure. You may have some bruising, swelling, or tenderness that resolves within a few weeks.

When will I see results?

You can see and feel improvement right away! Results continue to improve over the following 3-6 months as your skin heals and the area smooths out.

Is Avéli right for me?

Avéli is FDA-cleared for the treatment of cellulite dimples in the buttocks and thighs of adult women. A consultation with a trained provider can help determine if you're a good candidate.
