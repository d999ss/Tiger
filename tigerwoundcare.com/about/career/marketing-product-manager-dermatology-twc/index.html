Marketing Product Manager, Dermatology

Conshohocken, PA


Job Summary

Purpose

The Marketing Product Manager of Dermatology will support strategic initiatives acting as a key driver in the development and execution of marketing strategies. This role will serve as the voice of the business, execute marketing vision, developing project plans, and creating product solutions. Through close collaboration with cross-functional departments, the Marketing Product Manager will ensure that marketing efforts align with the organization’s strategy and business goals.

 

Responsibilities
 

  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to guide product development, ensuring alignment with market needs and customer expectations
  • Develop a deep understanding of industry trends to craft messaging, campaigns, collateral, and training materials that support both product launches and ongoing marketing initiatives
  • Lead sustaining projects for assigned product lines, including lifecycle management, product updates, and regulatory compliance
  • Assist with managing communications for social media strategies, public events, and other external engagements
  • Build relationships and coordinate meetings with key stakeholders to align efforts with the organization’s strategic initiatives Oversee projects from inception to completion, working closely with the Vice President of Marketing and other stakeholders to achieve project goals
  • Contribute to planning new company initiatives, such as launching new product ideas based on current and anticipated market trends
  • Prepare and present project updates, reports, and performance metrics
  • Analyze customer feedback and client insights to identify potential product improvements
  • Manage communications surrounding the lifecycle of Tiger Wound Care’s portfolio
  • Maintain a working knowledge of FDA 21 CFR 1271, FDA 21 CFR 820, AATB standards, ISO Standards, cGMP/CGTP, other relevant regulations/standards, and internal organizational policies and standard operating procedures
  • Travel domestically up to 30% to attend offsite meetings, conferences, and support business initiatives
  • Maintain acceptable attendance and punctuality for scheduled work hours and meetings
  • Ensure completion of assigned tasks and responsibilities within defined timeframes
  • Flexibility to work outside of normal business hours during weekdays or weekends with reasonable advance notice to support business/operational needs when necessary
  • Perform other duties as assigned

 

Skills

  • Strong project management skills demonstrated success in leading cross-functional teams, and an ability to drive projects to completion
  • Competency in market analysis and data interpretation to shape informed product strategies
  • Exceptional verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to effectively engage healthcare professionals
  • Effective time management, multitasking skills, and the ability to work both independently and as part of a team
  • Familiarity with manufacturing environments and validation procedures
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite
  • Excellent attention to detail and organization
  • Highest level of ethics and integrity
  • Ability to multi-task and work in a fast-paced environment

 

Qualifications/Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in business management, Marketing, or a relevant science field, required
  • Bachelor’s degree may be substituted to meet up to 4 years of experience requirements
  • 3–6 years of relevant experience in the medical devices and/or allografts field (wound care experience is preferred) Experience in Dermatology required
  • Experience in Tissue, Wound Care and Aesthetics preferred
  • Experience with project completion from start to finish
  • Clearance of favorable background investigation required
