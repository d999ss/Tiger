MicrotexturedSurface

Textured Surfaces are Not All the Same

More rough and aggressive shell surfaces have been classified as macrotextured. Sientras textured shell has been classified as a microtexture due to its less aggressive, finer surface.3 Sientras microtextured surface has been reported to have the lowest number of BIA-ALCL (cancer of the immune system) cases of all U.S. implant brands.3,13

Sientras microtextured shell surface3 and exclusive High-Strength Cohesive Gel are clinically shown to have low rates of capsular contracture, rupture, and reoperation.1

Scanning electron microscopic (SEM) images of Sientra and Allergan textured surfaces at 65x magnification. SEM is the industry standard to characterize the combination of shell surface disruption and pores promoting tissue adherence to the textured shell surface.
