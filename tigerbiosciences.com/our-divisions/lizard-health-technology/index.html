Turning Healthcare Visions

Lizard Health Technology GmbH, Germany

Lizard Health is a pioneering company specializing in medical device engineering and 3D printing.

We are experts in turning healthcare visions into reality, bringing the gap between innovative ideas and practical solutions in the medical field. At Lizard Health Technology, we provide comprehensive solutions that span the entire lifecycle of medical innovations, such as concept development, engineering and design, prototyping and testing, regulatory compliance assistance, manufacturing support, market access strategies and End-to-End Solutions.

By continuously pushing boundaries of what’s
possible in medical engineering and 3D printing,
Lizard Health aims to play a crucial role in
advancing healthcare technology and improving
patient care worldwide.

Medical Device Engineering

With years of exertise, we specialize in rapid, straightforward medical device engineering solutions. Our services encompass the entire development process, from initial concept to production and post-market surveillance.

With a focus on innovation we consistently surpass expectations. We work closely with Quality Management and Regulatory Affairs to ensure seamless compliance and exceptional outcomes.

3D Priniting

The availability of diverse 3D printing technologies and an extensive selection of materials empowers us to cater to a wide array of needs.

This encompasses areas such as constructing prototypes, fabricating instruments, developing implants, creating tools, and producing various devices.

Anatomical Models

Ideal for medical training programs and precise surgical planning. Enhance your teaching methods and treatment strategies with our realistic and customized solutions.
