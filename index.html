FAQ

You ask, we answer

What are CAMPs?

CAMPs (Cellular, Acellular, and Matrix-like Products) are advanced tissue technologies derived from placental tissue. They provide a natural scaffold for wound healing and tissue regeneration.

How long are products shelf-stable?

Our CAMPs products are shelf-stable for 2-5 years at room temperature, eliminating the need for refrigeration and simplifying inventory management.

Are your facilities accredited?

Yes. Both our Conshohocken, PA and San Antonio, TX processing facilities are AATB-accredited, meeting the highest standards for tissue banking and processing.

What makes Tiger BioSciences different?

We're the first fully vertically integrated tissue company, controlling every aspect from donor screening to distribution. This ensures unparalleled quality, accountability, and clinical confidence.
