The Power of Vertical Integration

What sets Tiger BioSciences apart is our unprecedented vertical integration. We don't just manufacture products—we oversee every single step of the process, ensuring unparalleled quality, accountability, and innovation.

Complete Tissue Recovery: We partner with trusted hospitals and donor programs to collect tissues under rigorous ethical, safety, and regulatory standards. Every donor is carefully screened, and every tissue is handled with respect and precision.

Advanced Processing: Our AATB-accredited facilities in Conshohocken, PA and San Antonio, TX house state-of-the-art processing capabilities. We employ the latest biotech methods to preserve tissue integrity while ensuring safety and efficacy.

Proprietary Research & Development: Our multidisciplinary R&D teams continuously push boundaries, developing next-generation CAMPs and medical devices that set new standards in regenerative medicine.

Global Distribution: Through our vertically integrated model, we maintain complete control from production to delivery, ensuring products arrive when needed, where needed.
