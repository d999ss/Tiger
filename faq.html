FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are CAMPs?

CAMPs (Cellular, Acellular, and Matrix-like Products) are advanced tissue technologies that support wound healing using placental tissue.

Are your facilities accredited?

Yes! Both our Conshohocken, PA and San Antonio, TX facilities are AATB-accredited.

How long are products shelf-stable?

Our CAMPs products are shelf-stable for 2-5 years at room temperature.
