THE SCIENCE

How Bellafill works

Bellafill is the only dermal filler that provides immediate and lasting results through a unique two-phase mechanism of action.

1

Immediate Volume

Smooth collagen gel provides immediate correction of smile lines or acne scars, with results you can see right away.

2

Collagen Stimulation

PMMA microspheres remain in place and stimulate your body's own collagen production for lasting, natural-looking results.

3

Long-Lasting Results

Your newly formed collagen surrounds the microspheres, creating a lasting foundation that provides continued improvement over time.
