Our Story

Tiger BioSciences represents the future of regenerative medicine and tissue technology. As the first fully vertically integrated tissue company, we control every aspect of our products—from donor screening and tissue recovery to processing, research and development, manufacturing, and distribution.

Our Distinct Advantage

Our comprehensive model ensures unparalleled quality, accountability, and confidence for both clinicians and patients. We oversee the complete tissue recovery process, from the moment of donation to arrival at medical facilities worldwide.

What Makes Tiger BioSciences Unique

We are the only company in the tissue technology industry that maintains complete control over every stage of the product lifecycle—from ethical tissue recovery through AATB-accredited processing, advanced R&D, and global distribution.

AATB Accreditation

Our processing facilities in Conshohocken, PA and San Antonio, TX are AATB-accredited, meeting the highest standards for tissue banking and processing. This accreditation demonstrates our commitment to:

  • Safety: Rigorous donor screening and testing protocols
  • Quality: Advanced processing methods that preserve tissue integrity
  • Regulatory Compliance: Full adherence to FDA and AATB standards
  • Traceability: Complete documentation from donor to recipient

Global Reach

With 16 specialized divisions across the United States, Germany, and Poland, Tiger BioSciences serves clinicians in multiple specialties including:

  • Wound care and podiatry
  • Plastic and reconstructive surgery
  • Orthopedics and sports medicine
  • Aesthetic and cosmetic medicine
  • General surgery

Innovation Through Research

Our RegenTX Labs division conducts ongoing research and development, including randomized controlled trials (RCTs), to validate the effectiveness of our technologies and advance the field of regenerative medicine. We collaborate with leading medical centers and research institutions to push the boundaries of what's possible in tissue engineering.
