The Tiger Way

The Power of Vertical Integration

Tiger BioSciences is unique in the tissue technology industry. We are the only company that maintains complete control over every stage of the product lifecycle—ensuring unmatched quality from recovery to recipient.

1

Tissue Recovery

We partner with trusted hospitals and donor programs to collect tissues under rigorous ethical, safety, and regulatory standards.

  • Birth Tissue Recovery: Ethical placental tissue collection
  • bioCARE: Comprehensive recovery services and donor coordination
  • Hospital Partnerships: Trusted relationships nationwide
  • Donor Screening: Rigorous eligibility criteria
2

Tissue Processing

Our AATB-accredited facilities process all tissues using advanced biomedical techniques that preserve natural ECM and growth factors.

  • RegenTX Conshohocken: Primary processing facility
  • RegenTX San Antonio: Large-scale operations
  • ISO Cleanrooms: Class 5/7 environments
  • cGMP Compliance: Quality manufacturing practices
3

Research & Development

Multidisciplinary research teams explore innovative materials and processing techniques to develop next-generation solutions.

  • RegenTX Labs: Advanced R&D facility
  • Clinical Trials: Ongoing RCTs for validation
  • Product Development: Next-gen CAMP technologies
  • Scientific Publications: Contributing to the field
4

Clinical Access & Distribution

Fast, precise, and reliable distribution with advanced packaging and expert handling ensures products reach clinicians safely.

  • Tiger Wound Care: Advanced CAMPs for healing
  • Tiger Aesthetics: Reconstructive solutions
  • Extremity Care: Specialized applications
  • Tiger International: Global distribution
